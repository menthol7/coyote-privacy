10.2 Responsible Gambling Practices

jixy strongly advocates for responsible gambling practices:

  • Set Financial Limits: Establish daily, weekly, and monthly betting limits before you start
  • Set Time Limits: Limit the time spent on betting activities
  • Never Chase Losses: Do not increase bet sizes to try to recover losses
  • Take Regular Breaks: Step away from betting regularly to maintain perspective
  • Avoid Betting Under Influence: Never bet while under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Don't Borrow to Bet: Never borrow money or use credit to fund betting activities
  • Keep Perspective: Remember that betting should be entertainment, not a way to make money
  • Monitor Your Behavior: Be aware of signs of problem gambling

10.3 Problem Gambling Warning Signs

Seek help immediately if you experience any of these warning signs:

  • Betting more money or time than you can afford
  • Chasing losses with bigger bets
  • Lying about gambling activities to family or friends
  • Neglecting work, family, or other responsibilities due to gambling
  • Feeling anxious, depressed, or angry about gambling losses
  • Using gambling as an escape from problems or negative feelings
  • Borrowing money to gamble or pay gambling debts
